# Influencer & Partnership Playbook

## Executive Summary

Influencer marketing remains the most effective crypto marketing strategy, with crypto influencers achieving 5.2% average engagement rates - far higher than traditional categories. This playbook provides a comprehensive framework for identifying, engaging, and managing Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs), building an affiliate program, and establishing strategic partnerships.

The crypto influencer industry generated $2.3 billion in commissions during 2024, with affiliate-driven user acquisition accounting for 35% of new registrations across major exchanges.

## KOL Tier System

### Tier Definitions

#### Mega KOLs (500K+ Followers)

**Profile:**
- Massive reach across crypto community
- High name recognition
- Typically cover broad crypto topics
- May have mainstream crossover appeal

**Characteristics:**
| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|---------------|
| Followers | 500K - 5M+ |
| Engagement Rate | 1-3% |
| Cost per Post | $10,000 - $100,000+ |
| Cost per Video | $20,000 - $150,000+ |
| Audience | Broad crypto, some mainstream |

**Best For:**
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Major announcements
- Credibility building
- Mainstream crossover

**Examples (Crypto):**
- Ran Neuner (Crypto Banter)
- Anthony Pompliano
- Raoul Pal
- BitBoy Crypto (controversial)

**Considerations:**
- High cost, lower engagement rate
- Audience may be less targeted
- Longer lead times
- More rigid content requirements
- Higher risk if controversy arises

---

#### Macro KOLs (100K - 500K Followers)

**Profile:**
- Significant reach within crypto niches
- Established credibility
- Often specialize in specific areas (DeFi, NFTs, trading)
- Active community engagement

**Characteristics:**
| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|---------------|
| Followers | 100K - 500K |
| Engagement Rate | 2-5% |
| Cost per Post | $2,500 - $10,000 |
| Cost per Video | $5,000 - $25,000 |
| Audience | Crypto-native, some specialization |

**Best For:**
- Product launches
- Feature promotions
- Educational content
- Sustained campaigns

**Considerations:**
- Good balance of reach and engagement
- More flexible on content
- Reasonable pricing
- May have loyal, trusting audience

---

#### Micro KOLs (10K - 100K Followers)

**Profile:**
- Highly engaged niche audiences
- Deep expertise in specific areas
- Strong community trust
- Often more authentic content

**Characteristics:**
| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|---------------|
| Followers | 10K - 100K |
| Engagement Rate | 4-8% |
| Cost per Post | $500 - $2,500 |
| Cost per Video | $1,000 - $5,000 |
| Audience | Highly targeted, niche |

**Best For:**
- Community building
- Authentic testimonials
- Niche market penetration
- Long-term partnerships

**Considerations:**
- Highest engagement rates
- Most cost-effective per engagement
- Often more creative freedom
- Can build genuine relationships
- Best for prediction market niche (sports, politics, etc.)

---

#### Nano KOLs (1K - 10K Followers)

**Profile:**
- Emerging voices in crypto
- Highly engaged small communities
- Often real users/enthusiasts
- Authentic and grassroots

**Characteristics:**
| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|---------------|
| Followers | 1K - 10K |
| Engagement Rate | 6-12% |
| Cost per Post | $100 - $500 |
| Cost per Video | $200 - $1,000 |
| Audience | Hyper-targeted, loyal |

**Best For:**
- Grassroots campaigns
- User-generated content
- Ambassador programs
- Volume plays

**Considerations:**
- Highest authenticity
- Lowest cost
- Requires volume for reach
- Great for ambassador programs
- May need more guidance

## Influencer Identification Criteria

### Evaluation Framework

#### 1. Audience Quality Score (40% weight)

| Criterion | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor |
|-----------|-----------|------|------|------|
| Fake follower % | <5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | >20% |
| Bot engagement % | <3% | 3-8% | 8-15% | >15% |
| Audience geo match | >80% target | 60-80% | 40-60% | <40% |
| Audience demo match | >70% match | 50-70% | 30-50% | <30% |

**Tools for Assessment:**
- SparkToro
- HypeAuditor
- Modash
- Upfluence
- Manual sampling

---

#### 2. Engagement Quality Score (30% weight)

| Criterion | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor |
|-----------|-----------|------|------|------|
| Engagement rate | >5% | 3-5% | 1-3% | <1% |
| Comment quality | Genuine discussion | Mixed | Generic | Bot-like |
| Reply rate | Frequent | Regular | Occasional | Rare |
| Sentiment | Positive | Neutral | Mixed | Negative |

**Engagement Rate Calculation:**
```
Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers x 100
```

**Advanced Engagement Analysis:**
- Review last 20 posts for consistency
- Check for engagement spikes (possible manipulation)
- Analyze comment sentiment and relevance
- Look for genuine conversations

---

#### 3. Content Quality Score (20% weight)

| Criterion | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor |
|-----------|-----------|------|------|------|
| Production value | Professional | Good | Basic | Poor |
| Educational value | Deep expertise | Informative | Surface | Misleading |
| Consistency | Daily+ | 4-5x/week | 2-3x/week | Irregular |
| Brand safety | Clean history | Minor issues | Some concerns | Red flags |

**Content Review Checklist:**
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule
- [ ] High-quality visuals/video
- [ ] Accurate information
- [ ] Engaging presentation style
- [ ] No history of promoting scams
- [ ] Professional communication

---

#### 4. Relevance Score (10% weight)

| Criterion | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor |
|-----------|-----------|------|------|------|
| Topic alignment | Core focus | Regular coverage | Occasional | Rare |
| Audience interest | High overlap | Moderate | Some | Minimal |
| Previous campaigns | Similar products | Related | Tangential | None |
| Platform match | Primary platform | Secondary | Minor | Mismatch |

---

### Red Flags to Avoid

**Immediate Disqualification:**
- History of promoting rug pulls or scams
- Fake follower percentage >25%
- Engagement manipulation evidence
- Regulatory violations or SEC issues
- Controversial political/social positions
- History of not disclosing paid promotions

**Warning Signs (Proceed with Caution):**
- Sudden follower spikes
- Inconsistent engagement patterns
- Generic, non-contextual comments
- Promotes too many projects
- Unrealistic claims in past promotions
- Poor communication during outreach

## Compensation Models

### Model 1: Flat Fee

**Structure:** Fixed payment per deliverable

**Typical Rates by Tier:**

| Tier | Twitter Post | Twitter Thread | YouTube Video | TikTok Video |
|------|--------------|----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Mega (500K+) | $5,000-15,000 | $8,000-20,000 | $15,000-50,000 | $10,000-30,000 |
| Macro (100-500K) | $1,500-5,000 | $2,500-8,000 | $5,000-15,000 | $3,000-10,000 |
| Micro (10-100K) | $300-1,500 | $500-2,500 | $1,000-5,000 | $500-3,000 |
| Nano (<10K) | $50-300 | $100-500 | $200-1,000 | $100-500 |

**Payment Terms:**
- 50% upfront, 50% on publication
- NET 30 after publication (for trusted partners)
- Crypto payment option (USDC preferred)

**Pros:**
- Simple and predictable
- Easy to budget
- Clear expectations

**Cons:**
- No performance incentive
- May overpay for poor results
- No long-term alignment

**Best For:**
- One-off campaigns
- Brand awareness
- Mega/Macro tier KOLs

---

### Model 2: Performance-Based (CPA)

**Structure:** Payment based on measurable results

**Typical CPA Rates:**

| Action | Typical CPA |
|--------|-------------|
| Signup (email) | $2-5 |
| Account creation | $5-15 |
| First deposit | $20-50 |
| First trade | $25-75 |
| Active trader (5+ trades) | $50-150 |

**Hybrid Structure Example:**
```
Base Fee: $2,000
+ $10 per signup (capped at 500)
+ $30 per first trade (uncapped)
+ $50 bonus per 100 active traders
```

**Tracking Requirements:**
- Unique referral links
- UTM parameters
- Promo codes
- Cookie tracking (30-day minimum)
- Real-time dashboard access

**Pros:**
- Aligned incentives
- Risk mitigation
- Scalable

**Cons:**
- Requires robust tracking
- KOLs may resist
- Harder to budget

**Best For:**
- Micro/Nano tier KOLs
- Long-term partnerships
- Ambassador programs

---

### Model 3: Token/Equity Allocation

**Structure:** Compensation in platform tokens or equity

**Token Allocation Framework:**

| Tier | Typical Allocation | Vesting Period |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| Mega KOL | 0.1-0.5% of tokens | 12-24 months |
| Macro KOL | 0.01-0.1% | 12-18 months |
| Micro KOL | 0.001-0.01% | 6-12 months |
| Advisors | 0.5-2% | 24-36 months |

**Vesting Structure Example:**
```
Total Allocation: 0.1% of tokens
Cliff: 6 months
Vesting: Linear over 18 months
Lock-up: 3 months post-TGE
```

**Token Compensation Benefits:**
- Aligned long-term incentives
- Cash preservation
- Genuine advocacy potential
- Upside sharing

**Considerations:**
- Securities law compliance (critical!)
- Token must have value proposition
- Clear vesting terms
- Tax implications for KOL

**Best For:**
- Advisory relationships
- Long-term ambassadors
- Strategic partnerships
- Pre-launch campaigns

---

### Model 4: Revenue Share/Affiliate

**Structure:** Ongoing percentage of referred user activity

**Typical Revenue Share Rates:**

| Activity | Commission Rate | Duration |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| Trading fees | 20-40% | Lifetime or 12 months |
| Deposits | 0.5-1% | First deposit |
| First trade volume | 0.1-0.5% | First trade only |

**Affiliate Program Tiers:**

| Tier | Requirements | Commission |
|------|--------------|------------|
| Bronze | 0-50 referrals | 25% |
| Silver | 51-200 referrals | 30% |
| Gold | 201-500 referrals | 35% |
| Platinum | 500+ referrals | 40% |
| Diamond | By invitation | 50%+ |

**Pros:**
- Ongoing relationship
- Passive income for KOL
- Long-term alignment

**Cons:**
- Complex tracking
- Delayed gratification
- Requires volume

**Best For:**
- Trading-focused KOLs
- Long-term partnerships
- Affiliate networks

---

### Compensation Negotiation Guidelines

**Starting Position:**
- Begin at 70% of your maximum budget
- Leave room for add-ons and bonuses
- Never reveal your full budget

**Negotiation Levers:**
- Payment timing (faster = lower rate)
- Exclusivity (exclusive = higher rate)
- Content ownership (rights = higher rate)
- Campaign duration (longer = volume discount)
- Additional deliverables (bundle deals)

**Walk-Away Points:**
- Rate exceeds 150% of tier average
- Demands for guaranteed results we can't verify
- Unreasonable content control
- No disclosure compliance
- History of similar product conflicts

## Campaign Types

### Campaign 1: Sponsored Content

**Description:** One-off paid promotion of platform/feature

**Deliverables:**
- Twitter post/thread
- YouTube video/integration
- Instagram post/story
- TikTok video
- Blog post

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

**Best For:** Product launches, feature announcements, awareness

**Example Campaign:**

```
CAMPAIGN BRIEF: Platform Launch

Objective: Drive awareness and signups for platform launch

KOL Selection:
- 2x Macro KOLs (crypto/trading focus)
- 5x Micro KOLs (sports/politics niches)
- 10x Nano KOLs (grassroots amplification)

Deliverables per KOL:
- 1 Twitter thread (10+ tweets)
- 1 follow-up engagement post
- Community Q&A participation

Key Messages:
1. Trade on real-world events
2. No house edge
3. Instant crypto settlement
4. [Signup bonus offer]

Timeline:
- Week 1: Macro KOL content
- Week 2: Micro + Nano amplification

Budget: $35,000
Expected Reach: 2M+ impressions
Target CPA: <$15
```

## FTC & Disclosure Requirements

### Legal Requirements Overview

**FTC Guidelines (United States):**
- All material connections must be disclosed
- Disclosures must be clear and conspicuous
- Violations can result in fines >$53,000 per incident
- Both brand and influencer can be held liable

**Key Regulations:**
- FTC Act Section 5 (Unfair/Deceptive Acts)
- FTC Endorsement Guides
- SEC regulations (for securities/tokens)
- Platform-specific rules

### Disclosure Requirements by Platform

#### Twitter/X

**Required:**
- #ad, #sponsored, or #partner in tweet
- Disclosure at beginning (not buried)
- Clear in threads (at least first and last tweet)

**Acceptable:**
```
#ad Excited to share my experience with @PlatformName -

```

```
Check out @PlatformName! Great platform for trading.
#crypto #trading #ad (buried at end)
```
