# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive go-to-market (GTM) strategy for launching a Polymarket-like prediction market platform. Drawing from successful crypto/DeFi launches including Blur's airdrop-driven growth, friend.tech's viral mechanics, and Polymarket's event-driven expansion, this strategy focuses on building sustainable growth through community engagement, strategic partnerships, and multi-channel marketing.

The prediction market industry generated over $44 billion in trading volume in 2024-2025, with projections reaching $95.5 billion by 2035 at a 47% CAGR. This presents a significant opportunity for well-positioned entrants.

## Target Audience Segmentation

- Limited prediction market options
- Poor liquidity on existing platforms
- Complex UX on current crypto platforms
- Seeking new alpha opportunities

**Acquisition Channels:**
- Crypto Twitter/X (primary)

- DeFi aggregators
- Crypto podcasts

## Launch Campaign Strategy

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch Hype Building (Weeks -8 to -1)

#### Week -8 to -6: Stealth & Seed

**Objectives:**
- Build initial community of 5,000+ members
- Generate organic buzz through insider access
- Identify and recruit early ambassadors

**Tactics:**

1. **Closed Alpha Invitation**
   - Invite-only access for 500 initial users
   - Target: crypto traders, prediction market enthusiasts
   - Referral system: 3 invites per user
   - Track and reward top referrers

2. **Stealth Discord Launch**
   - Private server for early adopters
   - Exclusive alpha channel with team access
   - Daily AMAs with founders
   - Feedback collection and implementation

3. **KOL Seeding**
   - Identify 50 tier-2/3 crypto KOLs
   - Provide early access and positions
   - No paid promotion yet - organic engagement
   - Build genuine relationships

4. **Content Foundation**
   - Launch blog with educational content
   - "How prediction markets work" series
   - Platform sneak peeks and feature reveals
   - SEO-optimized foundational articles

**Budget Allocation:** $25,000
- Community management: $8,000
- Content creation: $7,000
- KOL gifts/access: $5,000
- Design/assets: $5,000

---

#### Week -5 to -3: Building Anticipation

**Objectives:**
- Grow community to 25,000+ members
- Generate media coverage
- Create FOMO through exclusivity

**Tactics:**

1. **Points/Rewards Program Launch**
   - Pre-launch points for engagement
   - Leaderboard visibility
   - Clear hint at future token/airdrop potential
   - Daily tasks and challenges

2. **Testnet/Beta Launch**
   - Public beta with play money
   - Trading competitions
   - Bug bounty program
   - Feature voting

3. **Strategic Leaks**
   - Partnership hints
   - Funding news (if applicable)
   - Feature previews
   - "Coming soon" teasers

4. **Media Outreach**
   - Tier-2 crypto media coverage
   - Podcast appearances
   - Founder thought leadership
   - Industry commentary on current events

5. **Waitlist Campaign**
   - Email capture with referral mechanics
   - Position-in-line gamification
   - Early access for top referrers
   - Weekly updates to waitlist

**Budget Allocation:** $50,000
- Points program development: $15,000
- Media/PR: $15,000
- Paid waitlist ads: $10,000
- Community incentives: $10,000

---

#### Week -2 to -1: Final Countdown

**Objectives:**
- Community at 50,000+ members
- Waitlist at 100,000+ signups
- Maximum hype at launch

**Tactics:**

1. **Launch Date Announcement**
   - Coordinated social media blast
   - KOL amplification
   - Countdown timer on website
   - Email blast to waitlist

2. **Final Airdrop Criteria Reveal**
   - Clear criteria for early user rewards
   - Snapshot dates announced
   - Last-minute engagement surge

3. **Partnership Announcements**
   - Major partnership reveals
   - Integration announcements
   - Advisory board reveal

4. **Event Market Teaser**
   - Preview of launch day markets
